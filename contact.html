Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any course by clicking the "Enroll Now" button on the course page. You'll need to create an account or log in if you already have one. After payment processing, you'll get immediate access to the course materials.

Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our paid courses. To earn a certificate, you need to complete all course modules and pass the final assessment with a score of 80% or higher.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our support team within 30 days of enrollment for a full refund.

Are there any free courses available?

Yes, we offer several free introductory courses and a large collection of free resources. Check our "Resources" section for free learning materials, or look for courses marked as "Free" in our course catalog.
